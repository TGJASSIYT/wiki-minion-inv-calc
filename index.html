Welcome to the wiki for the Minion Inventory Calculator! This guide will help you understand how to use the calculator effectively and maximize its potential.

What is the Minion Inventory Calculator?

The Minion Inventory Calculator is a tool designed to help you calculate the number of real-life days it will take for a minion's inventory to fill up, considering factors like inventory slots, fuel, diamond spreading, and more.

How to Use

  1. Minion Inventory Slots

    Enter the total number of slots available in your minion's inventory. This is a required field. Each slot can hold up to 64 items.

  2. Backpack Slots (Optional)

    If your minion uses a backpack, enter the number of slots available in the backpack. This is optional and can be left blank if not applicable.

  3. Fuel Percentage (Optional)

    Enter the percentage of fuel boost applied to the minion. For example, if you’re using a fuel that gives a 20% boost, enter "20".

  4. Diamond Spreading

    Indicate whether your minion uses diamond spreading. This increases the effective production by 10%.

  5. Block Conversion

    Specify if your minion converts items into block form. If enabled, production will be divided by 160.

  6. Production in 1 Day

    Enter the total production amount your minion generates in one day. This is a required field.

  7. Calculate

    Click the "Calculate Days to Fill Inventory" button. The calculator will display:

    • The total days required to fill the inventory.
    • A breakdown of the time in years, months, and days (if applicable).
    • A note indicating that the calculated time is in real-life days.

Example

Let’s say you have a minion with the following settings:

  • Inventory Slots: 27
  • Backpack Slots: 5
  • Fuel Percentage: 20%
  • Diamond Spreading: Yes
  • Block Conversion: No
  • Production in 1 Day: 500 items

After filling in these fields and clicking "Calculate", the result will show the time required to fill the inventory in real-life days.

Common Issues

  • Error Message: "Please fill the required fields correctly."

    Ensure that you’ve entered valid values for Minion Inventory Slots and Production in 1 Day. These fields are required and cannot be zero or empty.

  • Note Missing: The real-life time note will only appear if all required fields are filled correctly.
